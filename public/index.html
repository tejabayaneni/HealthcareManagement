The department of gynaecology surgery deals with every form of gynaecological disorders that a woman might face at any point of her life like cases of endometriosis, pelvic inflammatory disease, ovarian cysts and uterine fibroids or polyps.All of these can be treated with laparoscopic surgeries.

The Department of Cardiology deals with heart and circulatory system diseases. With the latest technology, skilled physicians and compassionate staff provide care for abnormalities of cardiac rhythm and conduction, coronary artery disease, cardiomyopathies, heart failure and valvular heart disease.
