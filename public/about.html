Welcome to About us

Appointment scheduler system is to computerize the front office management of patients and doctors which is user friendly , cost effective and simple to use. Appointment scheduler system deals with managing patients and doctors appointments. This application will be useful for patients to find the available timings of the concerned doctors and schedule an appointment with the concerned doctor. It contains the details of the patients issues as described by the patients. The Appointment scheduler system can be entered using a username and password. Appointment scheduler system is accessible by patient and doctor. The data is well protected for personal use and makes the data processing very fast and easy. This application restricts the patient to make only one appointment with a doctor on a given day.
